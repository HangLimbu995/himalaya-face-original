Renowned for our superior trekking apparel, our Nepalese factory produces attire that is comfortable, strong, and affordable. We use only the highest quality, durable fabrics, combined with expert local craftsmanship, to create products that are ideal for all trekking environments.

Committed to Excellence in Trekking Apparel Manufacturing

With over 20 years of operation in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal, our esteemed factory prides itself on expertly tailoring each garment with meticulous attention to detail. We employ skilled local artisans who utilize durable, high-performance fabrics to ensure exceptional quality in every piece.

We uphold a steadfast commitment to responsible and sustainable production practices, prioritizing fair treatment of all our workers. Rigorous inspections of our supply chain are conducted regularly to uphold high ethical and environmental standards.

FAQ

"Got questions? Get answers here."

Do you have an MOQ?

We do work with MOQ’s, because we do mass production and buy newly made materials directly from the mill. It’s not possible to offer good supply of materials and stable production quality without meeting the minimums. Minimums depend on the fabric, but on average we will need around 500 pieces per style per colour to start with.

Where are you guys located?

We are located in Kathmandu, Nepal. And central hub for tourist.

How do we get started?

You can reach out to use via our contact page. Please tell us a bit more about your brand, your selling format, your products and so on. If it looks like we are the right clothing manufacturer for your brand we can start out with some costing exercise and trial orders.

To which countries do you ship?

We are new on export so we ship all the available possible countries. And we work on FOB terms and do not offer DDP OR FCL terms.

What are your payment terms?

The terms are 30% deposit and 70% T/T against docs.
